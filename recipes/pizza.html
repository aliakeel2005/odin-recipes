pizza

Description

you will learn how to make and bake a delicous pizza from scratch

why buy it when you can make it? we will start from the dough

ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • meats
  • veggies
  • cheese
  • tomato paste

Steps

  1. Add 3 cups of flour to a medium sized bowl
  2. Add 2 cups of water to the flour
  3. Knead with hand or machine until its soft
  4. Spread the dough in a circle
  5. Add tomato paste
  6. Add your meats and veggies then cheese respectivley
  7. Bake on medium-high heat for 45 minutes
